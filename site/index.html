AN EXPERIENCE INSIDE BEAUTIFUL BUILDINGS IN GHENT

How?

Scan the QR-codes in front of the buildings. You will get a glimps of the atmosphere inside.

What is 'Take A Look Inside'?

Ghent has all kinds of beautiful buildings. But sometimes they aren't accessible for visitors. Don't you want to know what's behind the walls? Go ahead and scan all the buildings on the map.

Why should I download?

Get a personal home screen with a Must See recomendation list, your own favorites and Look Laters and a list of all the buildings you've already scanned.
